ABOUT US

This is a collaborative effort by the team ShiRaPaM including Shivani Seghal, Rafaeatul Kabir, Paulo Kawakami and Mayowa Mosuro for the python project. Our Django-based website offers a seamless user experience, allowing easy registration and login for personalized access. Users can choose one or more topics of interest to tailor their quiz experience, engaging with a dynamic system that generates Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) based on their selections. Instant results provide efficient feedback, while comprehensive quiz history tracking allows users to revisit and reflect on their progress. The platform is equipped with a robust Django admin panel, empowering administrators to effortlessly manage and enhance the website. Admins can add new topics and questions to keep the content current, and access users' quiz records for effective monitoring and support. Shirapam Quiz Hub is not just a learning platform; it's a community-driven initiative to make education interactive, enjoyable, and accessible, inviting users to join us on this educational journey where knowledge meets innovation and success is achieved through engaging quizzes.
